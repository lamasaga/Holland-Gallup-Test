行动 / Activator（影响力 / Influencing）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：“行动”才干主题突出的人善于把想法变成行动。他们渴望立刻行动，而不只是空谈。
People exceptionally talented in the Activator theme can make things happen by turning thoughts into action. They want to do things now, rather than simply talk about them.

特征 / Feature：能够把想法化为行动，让事情发生；他们想做就做，不愿只停留在讨论。
can make things happen by turning thoughts into action. They want to do things now, rather than simply talk about them.

应用 / Application：如果“行动”是你的前五才干之一，你很擅长动员团队行动起来：你清楚地传达需要做什么，并营造紧迫感。你是为组织节省时间、精力和成本的催化剂。当项目停滞不前时，你会推动事情前进。你不害怕冒险，这让你成为一个出色的创新者。你的能量和以行动为导向的方式能带动势头，让事情持续推进。
With Activator in your Top 5, you likely excel at mobilising your team to action — you communicate what has to be done and then instill a sense of urgency. You are a catalyst for saving your organization time, energy and money. You are impatient for action and get things moving when projects stall. You are not afraid to take risks, which can make you a great innovator. Your energy and action-oriented approach instill momentum — you keep things progressing forward.

盲点 / Blind Spots：你可能倾向于在没有具体计划的情况下就冲出去行动。在行动前，先停下来考虑替代方案和可能的后果。 你急于开始，可能会错过别人的重要想法。给团队中的其他人表达意见的机会。 因为你相信只有行动才能带来成果，别人可能会觉得你缺乏条理或冲动。如果你是管理者或团队领导，花时间解释你的思路，并确保大家对期望有清晰的了解。 你启动事情很快，这可能让其他人感到被忽视、不被欣赏或不被重视。花时间倾听别人，确保方向正确。 虽然有时大家都需要在没有周密计划的情况下勇往直前，但在做重大决定前，考虑各种选择并权衡利弊总是一个好习惯。 你催促别人采取行动的力度，可能会让一些人感到不堪重负，被迫在准备好之前行动。拥有较强“行动”才干的人要记住，别人不一定有同样的紧迫感，有些人需要更多时间思考后再行动。 如果“行动”不是你的突出才干，也不意味着你缺乏主动性或激情。你可能只是更喜欢在行动前仔细考虑。当需要立即行动时，尽量在自己熟悉或擅长的领域发挥。找到能帮助你启动事情的其他才干，比如“责任”“信仰”或“包容”，它们也能为你点燃行动的火花。有时你需要给自己设定一个合理的标准，然后立刻去做。
You may have a tendency to charge ahead and act without a concrete plan. Challenge yourself to stop and consider alternatives and consequences before taking action. Your drive to get started means you may miss important ideas from others. Give others on the team a chance to give input. Because you believe only action leads to performance, you may be perceived as unorganised or impulsive. If you are a manager or team leader, take time to explain your reasoning and make sure you are clear about your expectations. You start things quickly and that may cause others to feel left out, unappreciated or undervalued. Take time to listen to others to ensure you are headed in the right direction. There are times everyone needs to charge ahead and act without a solid plan. Before making a major decision, it is always a good practice to consider alternatives and weigh options first. The force of their urging people to take action might make others feel overwhelmed and pressured to act before they are comfortable. People with strong Activator talents should remember that others may not feel that same urgency, and some people will need more time to think before they act. If Activator Is a Lesser Theme for You If you lack the intensity of Activator talents, it does not mean you lack initiative, fire or the ability to get things started. Rather, you might be someone who prefers to consider actions carefully before taking them. Ensure that you are operating in areas of familiarity or expertise if the situation requires immediate action. Know the best way to prepare yourself to take action. Consider the significant decisions you’ve made in your life and determine what steps you went through to feel ready to take action. Did you talk to others first to get their opinions? Did you thoroughly research the topic area? Did you systematically evaluate different scenarios and obstacles? Among your top themes, find those that help you get things started. Responsibility , Belief or Includer talents could provide the spark of initiative for you in the same way Activator talents do for others. Sometimes you might have to set a reasonable standard for yourself and just do it. 特征 / Feature：喜欢迎接结识新朋友并赢得他们好感的挑战。他们从打破僵局、与人建立联系中获得满足。
love the challenge of meeting new people and winning them over. They derive satisfaction from breaking the ice and making a connection with someone.

应用 / Application：你天生友好，能迅速让别人放松下来，这让别人愿意和你一起工作。你天生的社交能力能创造连接，而你可以把这些连接分享出去——当你和别人交谈时，你常常能把他们介绍给能帮助到他们的人。 你的关系网常常成为团队遇到困难时的解决资源。你搭建桥梁的能力，可以帮助团队与其他部门和群体建立联系。
You are naturally friendly and quickly set people at ease. This makes others want to work with you. Your natural networking ability creates connections that you can share — when you talk with others, you often connect them with people who can help them. Your network is often a source of solutions when your team gets stuck and can't find the answer. Your bridge-building capability can be an asset in connecting your team with other departments and constituencies.

Your need for making numerous connections can cause others to see you as superficial and less than genuine in your relationships. You are quick to praise others. Be sure your praise is authentic, deserved and individualised. Some people might find your high energy and natural enthusiasm off-putting. You may have a tendency to reveal yourself and trust others too quickly. This may cause more reserved people to step back from a relationship. 标准定义 / Standard Definition：“竞争”才干主题突出的人通过与他人比较来衡量自己的进步。他们渴望获胜，享受竞争带来的刺激。

特征 / Feature：以他人的表现作为衡量自己进步的标准。他们力争第一，沉醉于竞赛之中。
measure their progress against the performance of others. They strive to win first place and revel in contests.

You create daily, monthly and yearly metrics for success to track your performance scores. This creates an energy for your team that pushes them to reach higher levels of achievement. Your drive to outperform others can stimulate innovation and creativity. You are naturally attuned to real-world measures that assess achievement, leading you to describe what outstanding performance looks like. Your drive to win and your need for clear metrics that define success can inspire others and cause them to want to be on your team.

盲点 / Blind Spots：如果你是团队领导，和团队成员竞争只会适得其反。相反，找到方法用“竞争”才干来定义并庆祝团队的胜利。 你的“竞争”才干推动你，也可能让你推动别人——有时力度过大。记住，不惜一切代价的胜利不是胜利，而是自我挫败。在鞭策自己和团队取胜时，要确保自己的正直品格不受影响。 不要让竞争精神影响你与团队成员和同事的关系。找到每个人的强项，把他们放在最能发挥作用的位置，形成获胜的组合。 不是每个人都有你这样的求胜动力，不断把自己的表现和别人比较，可能会冒犯或伤害他们。记住，取胜不能以牺牲原则为代价。
If you lead a team, competing to win against your team is counter-productive. Instead, find a way to use your Competition to define and celebrate team wins. Your Competition talents push you, so you might tend to push others — sometimes too hard. Remember that winning at all costs isn't winning; it's defeating yourself. Make sure your integrity remains intact when you push yourself and your team to victory. Don't let your competitive spirit cloud your relationships with your team members and peers. Find ways to unite your team members by finding what each does best and positioning them for maximum effectiveness to create a winning combination. Be careful not to let your competitive spirit get in the way of your relationships. Not everyone has your drive to win, and constantly comparing your performance to theirs might offend them or hurt their feelings. Winning at all costs isn’t winning; it’s self-defeating. Make sure you maintain your principles when you push yourself toward ultimate victory.
