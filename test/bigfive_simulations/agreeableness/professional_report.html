B. 主题叙事 | Theme Narrative

说明 / Note：以下主题叙事已提供中英双语对照：中文翻译优先保留本土教练式表达，英文保留 Gallup CliftonStrengths 原有表述风格。每个主题依次列出标准定义、特征、应用建议与盲点。

交往 / Relator（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：交往能力强的人喜欢人际间的亲密关系。 他们最大的满足是与朋友一道为实现一个目标而共同努力。
People exceptionally talented in the Relator theme enjoy close relationships with others. They find deep satisfaction in working hard with friends to achieve a goal.

特征 / Feature：交往能力强的人享受亲密的人际关系。他们最大的乐趣是与朋友一起合作，共同实现共同目标。
You enjoy close, intimate relationships. Your greatest pleasure comes from working alongside friends toward a shared goal.

应用 / Application：愿意与他人分享知识和经验，支持他人的成长和发展。 尊重和接纳他人的不同观点、文化和背景。倡导多样性和包容性，并与不同背景的人合作，增进相互理 解和合作。 重视他人的观点和感受。倾听他人的需求和问题，并展现出真正的关心。 培养良好的社交技巧，学会察言观色，适应不同的人际交往风格，以更好地理解和与他人沟通。 培养解决问题的技巧，处理冲突时保持冷静和客观，寻求妥协和解决方案，有效沟通，寻求共同的利益 和理解。
Be willing to share your knowledge and experience with others to support their growth and development. Respect and accept others' different viewpoints, cultures, and backgrounds. Advocate for diversity and inclusion, and work with people from diverse backgrounds to increase mutual understanding and cooperation. Value others' perspectives and feelings. Listen to their needs and problems and show genuine care. Develop good social skills. Learn to read the room and adapt to different interpersonal styles so you can better understand and communicate with others. Build problem-solving skills. Stay calm and objective when handling conflict, seek compromise and solutions, communicate effectively, and look for shared interests and understanding.

盲点 / Blind Spots：· 过于坚持己见和固定的交往模式，不愿意适应和调整自己的行为和观念，影响交往的互动性和流畅度。 · 忽视寻找共同的利益点和共同目标，导致交往关系的表面化和短暂性。 · 不尊重他人的个人空间和隐私，过于侵入性的行为和过问，造成他人的不适和疏离。
· You may cling too tightly to your own views and fixed ways of relating, resisting adapting or adjusting your behavior and beliefs, which can make interactions less fluid. · You may neglect to find shared interests and common goals, causing relationships to become superficial and short-lived. · You may fail to respect others' personal space and privacy, behaving intrusively or asking overly personal questions, which can make others uncomfortable and distant.

包容 / Includer（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：包容力强的人善于接纳他人。他们关心那些被忽略的人， 并努力让这些人融入集体。
People exceptionally talented in the Includer theme accept others. They show awareness of those who feel left out and make efforts to include them.

特征 / Feature：包容力强的人是慈善家，他们善于接纳他人，关心被忽视的人，并促使他们融入集体。
You are naturally accepting, caring about those who are overlooked and helping them become part of the group.

应用 / Application：展现对不同背景、观点和经验的尊重和欣赏。认识到每个人都有独特的贡献和价值，倾听并接纳不同意 见和观点。 主动提供支持和帮助他人的机会，展现关心和同理心。愿意倾听他人的需求，并努力为他人提供支持和 协助。 鼓励积极的沟通和反馈，提供安全和开放的环境，让每个人都能表达意见和感受。确保沟通渠道畅通， 包括定期会议、团队讨论和个人反馈。 加强自我意识，了解自己的偏好和偏见，积极反思和调整自己的行为，加强与他人的情感连接和共情能 力。
Show respect and appreciation for different backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences. Recognize that everyone has unique contributions and value, and listen to and accept differing opinions and views. Actively offer opportunities to support and help others, showing care and empathy. Be willing to listen to others' needs and do your best to provide support and assistance. Encourage positive communication and feedback by providing a safe and open environment where everyone can express opinions and feelings. Make sure communication channels stay open, including regular meetings, team discussions, and personal feedback. Strengthen self-awareness by understanding your own preferences and biases, reflecting actively, and adjusting your behavior to build stronger emotional connections and empathy with others.

盲点 / Blind Spots：· 对于不同文化背景、价值观和行为规范的认知不足，缺乏对多元文化的尊重和理解。 · 与同质化的社交圈子保持较高的亲密度，缺乏与多样化背景的人交流和接触的机会。 · 身处不支持包容和多样性的环境，如歧视性言论和行为较多的地方，会对包容主题产生负面影响。
· You may lack knowledge of different cultural backgrounds, values, and behavioral norms, leading to insufficient respect and understanding of diversity. · You may stay closely connected to a homogeneous social circle and miss opportunities to interact with people from diverse backgrounds. · Being in an environment that does not support inclusion and diversity, such as one with frequent discriminatory words or actions, can weaken your Includer theme.

和谐 / Harmony（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：“和谐”才干主题突出的人渴求协调一致。 他们避免冲突，寻求共识。
People exceptionally talented in the Harmony theme look for consensus. They don't enjoy conflict; rather, they seek areas of agreement.

特征 / Feature：和谐主题突出的人追求协调与共识，像和平天使一样化解冲突、营造和谐。
You pursue coordination and consensus, resolving conflict and creating harmony like a peacemaker.

应用 / Application：确保与周围人之间有开放、透明和及时的沟通渠道，避免信息不对称和误解，增强合作和和谐。 重视他人的观点和感受，积极倾听他人的想法和建议，展示出真诚的关怀和关心。 鼓励团队成员之间的合作和协作，强调团队的共同目标和利益，共同努力解决问题和实现目标。 处理冲突时采取积极的方法，鼓励开放的对话和理解，寻求妥协和共赢的解决方案，以维护和谐的关 系。
Make sure open, transparent, and timely communication channels exist with the people around you. Avoid information gaps and misunderstandings, and strengthen cooperation and harmony. Value others' perspectives and feelings. Listen actively to their ideas and suggestions and show sincere care and concern. Encourage teamwork and collaboration among team members, emphasizing shared goals and interests so everyone works together to solve problems and achieve objectives. Approach conflict constructively by encouraging open dialogue and understanding, seeking compromise and win-win solutions, and preserving harmonious relationships.

盲点 / Blind Spots：· 合理的冲突可以带来创新和改进，过度追求和谐可能忽视了冲突的积极作用。 · 可能导致忽视个人的需求和边界，无法有效地满足自己的利益和利益。 · 压抑自己的情绪和需求，可能使得问题无法得到解决和满足。
· Healthy conflict can drive innovation and improvement; pursuing harmony too strongly may overlook the positive role of conflict. · It may lead you to neglect your own needs and boundaries, making it hard to protect your own interests. · Suppressing your own emotions and needs may prevent problems from being resolved and needs from being met.

关联 / Connectedness（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：“关联”才干主题突出的人深信世间万物都彼此关联。 他们相信，世间没有巧合，凡事必有成因。
People exceptionally talented in the Connectedness theme have faith in the links among all things. They believe there are few coincidences and that almost every event has meaning.

特征 / Feature：关联主题突出的人坚信世界万物相互关联，认为一切都有其成因和联系。
You believe that everything in the world is connected and that every event has its cause and purpose.

应用 / Application：努力理解他人的需求、意见和观点，并尝试找到共同利益和解决方案。 与不同部门和团队的人合作，促进跨部门的合作、协调和促进信息共享，保证项目的顺利进行。 积极参与行业内的网络活动和社交场合。与同行、专家和领导者建立联系，通过交流和分享经验来扩大 影响力和资源。 与他人分享自己的知识、经验和资源，帮助他人提升技能和知识水平。
Strive to understand others' needs, opinions, and perspectives, and look for shared interests and solutions. Work with people across departments and teams to promote collaboration, coordination, and information sharing so projects run smoothly. Actively participate in networks and social events. Connect with peers, experts, and leaders to expand your influence and resources through exchange and shared experience. Share your knowledge, experience, and resources with others to help them grow their skills and understanding.

盲点 / Blind Spots：· 追求关联而忽视事实和逻辑的支持。将不相关的事物强行关联起来，导致关联的无效性和误导性。 · 忽视潜在的相关性，而未能发现事物之间的关联。这种遗漏可能导致对问题的理解不完整，无法获得全面的 洞察。 · 关联主题往往涉及多个因素和变量，可能过于关注某些因素，而忽视了其他重要的因素。这种偏重可能导致 对问题的解释和预测的不准确性。
· You may pursue connections while neglecting factual and logical support, forcing unrelated things together and producing invalid or misleading links. · You may overlook potential connections and miss relationships between things, leading to incomplete understanding and less comprehensive insight. · Connectedness often involves many factors and variables; you may focus too much on certain factors while ignoring others, reducing the accuracy of your explanations and predictions.

积极 / Positivity（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：积极的人浑身充满了富有感染力的热情。 他们乐观向上，并能感召周围的人。
People exceptionally talented in the Positivity theme have an enthusiasm that is contagious. They are upbeat and can get others excited about what they are going to do.

特征 / Feature：积极的人散发着富有感染力的热情，以快乐和积极向上的态度激励周围的人。
You radiate contagious enthusiasm, inspiring those around you with joy and a positive, forward-looking attitude.

应用 / Application：关注自己内心的对话，并将其转化为积极的、励志的声音。培养自信和自尊，相信自己的能力和潜力。 以积极的心态对待工作中的挑战和困难。寻找解决问题的方法和机会，抱有成长的心态，相信自己能够 克服困难并取得成功。 与积极向上的人为伍，建立支持和积极的人际关系。分享积极的经历和观点，与他人共享快乐和成功， 鼓励和激励他人。 注重自己的身心健康，保持良好的生活习惯。合理安排工作和休息时间，参与锻炼、放松和充电的活 动，保持积极的生活态度。
Pay attention to your inner dialogue and turn it into a positive, encouraging voice. Build confidence and self-respect by believing in your abilities and potential. Approach challenges and difficulties with a positive mindset. Look for ways to solve problems and seize opportunities. Adopt a growth mindset and believe you can overcome obstacles and succeed. Surround yourself with positive people and build supportive, uplifting relationships. Share positive experiences and perspectives, spread joy and success with others, and encourage and inspire them. Take care of your physical and mental health and maintain good habits. Balance work and rest, exercise, relax, and recharge so you can keep a positive attitude toward life.

盲点 / Blind Spots：· 过于积极的人可能忽视现实中的问题和困难，对挑战和障碍持过于乐观的态度，导致缺乏实际的解决方案。 · 有时候，过于强调积极的态度可能导致表面上的积极，而忽视真实的情感和需求，造成内心的不满和不适。 · 积极主题的人可能过于追求成功和进步，过度竞争和自我比较，导致焦虑和不满足感。
· Being overly positive may cause you to overlook real problems and difficulties, approach challenges with excessive optimism, and fail to develop practical solutions. · Sometimes emphasizing a positive attitude too strongly can create a surface-level positivity that ignores real emotions and needs, causing inner dissatisfaction and discomfort. · People with strong Positivity may pursue success and progress so intensely that they become overly competitive and compare themselves too much with others, leading to anxiety and a sense of inadequacy.

伯乐 / Developer（关系建立 / Relationship Building）

标准定义 / Standard Definition：“伯乐”才干主题突出者善于发现并培养他人的潜能。 他们能够察觉任何微小的进步，并从中得到满足感。
People exceptionally talented in the Developer theme recognize and cultivate the potential in others. They spot the signs of each small improvement and derive satisfaction from evidence of progress.

特征 / Feature：欣赏他人潜力并善于发现进步的人，喜悦于细微的成长，激励他人不断进步。
You appreciate others' potential, notice even small improvements, and find joy in every sign of growth as you encourage people to keep progressing.

应用 / Application：观察和评估身边的人，发现他们的优点、才能和潜力，注意他们的成就和特长。 与他人建立良好的沟通和合作关系，了解他们的目标和需求，帮助他们发展他们的潜力，并在实现目标 的过程中提供指导和建议。 通过认可和赞赏，激发他人的动力和自信心。表达对他们的信任，并鼓励他们尝试新的挑战和机会。 通过良好的领导实践和积极的影响力，发展自己的领导能力，成为他人的榜样和引导者。 与他人建立导师关系，分享经验和知识，帮助他们发展专业能力和职业发展。
Observe and evaluate the people around you to discover their strengths, talents, and potential. Notice their achievements and special abilities. Build good communication and working relationships with others. Understand their goals and needs, help them develop their potential, and offer guidance and suggestions as they work toward their objectives. Recognize and praise others to spark their motivation and confidence. Express your trust in them and encourage them to try new challenges and opportunities.
